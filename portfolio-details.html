Bhagvat Homoeopathy

Project Overview

For Bhagvat Homoeopathy, a reputable clinic in Surat, we had the privilege of designing a complete brand identity package tailored to the vision of Dr. Sahil Patel, the clinic's owner. Dr. Patel envisioned a logo that would not only represent his practice but also resonate with the core values of homeopathy. We meticulously followed his brief, delivering a logo that encapsulates his philosophy and meets his expectations precisely.

In addition to the logo, we designed a visiting card that complements the clinic's brand identity. The card features the clinic's name, tagline, address, phone number, and email, all presented in a clear and elegant font. The use of a white background with contrasting black text ensures a professional and polished appearance. Dr. Sahil Patel was thoroughly satisfied with the final design, appreciating its simplicity and effectiveness in conveying the clinic's image.

As a doctor, I recently had the privilege of working with an exceptionally talented graphics designer to craft the perfect logo for my clinic. final product that exceeded all expectations.

Dr Sahil Patel

BHMS.MD (Scholar)

To further extend the clinic's branding, we utilized the elements from the visiting card to create a cohesive banner. By maintaining the same font and color scheme, we ensured brand consistency across all materials. The banner design was intentionally kept simple and clean, emphasizing key information while avoiding unnecessary clutter. Drawing inspiration from a selection of homoeopathy clinic banner templates, we crafted a design that is both professional and aligned with industry standards.

Dr. Sahil Patel expressed his appreciation for the unified branding across all materials, which effectively communicates his clinic's identity to patients and the community.
